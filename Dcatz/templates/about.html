About Us

Welcome to D'Catz, your number one source for all things cat-related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by the IT students of Dalubhasaan ng Lungsod ng Lucena, D'Catz has come a long way from its beginnings from scratch. When their team first started out, their passion for this industry drove them to create something unique, and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Quezon Province area, and are thrilled to be a part of the dynamic wing of the cat industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
BSIT students
